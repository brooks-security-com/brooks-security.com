As 2024 kicks off, I find myself facing a set of ambitious but absolutely necessary goals that I cannot afford to put off any longer. This year, I’m committing to a challenging, purposefully selected sequence of professional certifications, each one a stepping stone designed to systematically broaden my expertise and solidify my role as a knowledgeable leader in the realms of IT Security and Cloud Security.

My approach isn’t just about collecting acronyms for my résumé. Instead, it’s an intentional journey: every certification targets a specific area where I want to deepen my skillset, sharpen real-world problem-solving abilities, and gain credentials that speak to both technical competence and strategic understanding. I’ve identified key milestones that align with current industry needs and my long-term vision of becoming a top-tier expert in security, architecture, and cloud solutions.

This personal mission is not just about passing exams; it’s about transformation. By the end of 2024, I aim to have built not only a portfolio of respected certifications but also a foundation of practical knowledge and confidence that will accelerate my path as a professional in this evolving and vital field.

## 2024 - A Year of Intensive Learning

### AWS Solutions Architect Associate - January
Starting the year strong, I'm diving into the AWS Solutions Architect Associate certification. It’s all about mastering the art of designing and managing scalable systems on AWS.

### AWS Certified Security Specialist - February
Security is awesome, and cloud is awesome! This certification is all about deepening my knowledge in securing AWS platforms. It's crucial for my goal of becoming a cloud security architect.

### Red Hat Certified Specialist in Security - March
March brings a focus on Red Hat's take on security. This will complement my AWS expertise, offering a broader perspective on system security.

### CISSP - June
The CISSP is the crown jewel of IT security certifications. It's a comprehensive deep dive into information security and will be my most challenging yet rewarding undertaking.

### Red Hat Certified Specialist in Performance Tuning - July
Performance tuning is an artform. This certification will help me fine-tune systems for optimal performance.

### Red Hat Certified Specialist in Service Management - August
Service management is key in HPCs and Datacenters (the backbone of cloud). This certification will sharpen my skills in managing and automating services effectively.

### Red Hat Certified Specialist in Diagnostics and Troubleshooting - September
Troubleshooting is where theory meets reality. This certification is all about honing my diagnostic skills to keep systems running smoothly.

### Red Hat Certified Specialist in Identity Management - October
Identity management is a critical aspect of security. This certification will enhance my ability to manage identities securely and efficiently.

### The RHCA Certification Finale
All of the Red Hat certs on this list are just hurdles toward the Red Hat Certified Architect (RHCA) certification. It's the ultimate goal for 2024. 

## The Journey Ahead

2024 is not just about racking up certs; it's my ultimate year of growth, challenge, and professional development. Each certification is a piece of the puzzle, contributing to my goal of becoming a leading expert in IT security and cloud solutions.

Ill be posting my wins and losses to this blog as I go.
