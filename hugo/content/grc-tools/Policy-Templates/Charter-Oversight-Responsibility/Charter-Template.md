9|## Purpose

11|This Charter establishes the formal oversight responsibilities for internal controls over financial reporting, information security, and operational compliance at ____.
12|
13|## Scope
14|
15|This Charter applies to the governing body (e.g., Board of Directors, Audit Committee) and executive management responsible for oversight of internal controls across the organization.
16|
17|## Charter
18|
19|### Oversight Body
20|
21|The ____ (hereafter "the Oversight Body") is responsible for overseeing:
22|
23|1. **Internal Control Environment:** Ensuring management has designed and implemented effective internal controls over financial reporting, information security, and operational processes.
24|2. **Risk Management:** Reviewing and approving the organization's risk appetite, risk assessment methodology, and risk treatment plans.
25|3. **Compliance:** Overseeing compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.
26|4. **Audit:** Engaging with internal and external auditors, reviewing audit findings, and ensuring management addresses identified deficiencies.
27|
28|### Responsibilities
29|
30|The Oversight Body must:
31|
32|- Review and approve the organization's Information Security Program (ISP) on at least an annual basis.
33|- Receive regular reports from the ____ (CISO/Security Officer) on the state of information security controls.
34|- Ensure adequate resources are allocated for information security and compliance programs.
35|- Review significant security incidents and management's response.
36|- Approve exceptions to security policies that present material risk to the organization.
37|- Ensure management maintains a Plan of Action and Milestones (POA&M) for identified control deficiencies.
38|
39|### Management Responsibilities
40|
41|Executive management is responsible for:
42|
43|- Designing, implementing, and maintaining effective internal controls.
44|- Providing the Oversight Body with accurate and timely information about the state of controls.
45|- Remediating control deficiencies within agreed timeframes.
46|- Fostering a culture of compliance and ethical behavior.
47|
48|### Meeting Cadence
49|
50|The Oversight Body must meet at least ____ (quarterly/semi-annually) to review:
51|
52|- Information security program performance
53|- Risk assessment updates
54|- Audit findings and remediation status
55|- Security incidents and lessons learned
56|- Changes in the regulatory landscape
57|
58|### Reporting
59|
60|The Oversight Body must maintain written records of its meetings, decisions, and recommendations. These records must be retained for a minimum of ____ years.
61|
62|## Roles and Responsibilities
63|
64|| Role | Responsibility |
65||------|----------------|
66|| Oversight Body (Board/Audit Committee) | Strategic oversight; risk appetite approval; annual ISP review |
67|| CEO / Executive Management | Implementation of controls; resource allocation; fostering compliance culture |
68|| ____ (CISO/Security Officer) | ISP management; reporting to Oversight Body; incident response oversight |
69|
70|## Compliance and Enforcement
71|
72|This Charter is binding on all members of the Oversight Body and executive management. Failure to fulfill oversight responsibilities may result in action by the full Board or regulatory authorities.
