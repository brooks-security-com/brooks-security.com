# AI Usage Policy

Effective Date: ____
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

This policy outlines the acceptable use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies by ____ Personnel. It serves as a specific extension of the Data Protection Policy, emphasizing best practices, data sensitivity, and compliance requirements for AI systems.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Personnel who use AI tools and services in the course of their work, including generative AI, AI-enabled development tools, and AI services integrated into approved platforms.

## Policy

### Approved AI Platforms

- All AI tasks involving sensitive or customer data must utilize only organization-approved AI platforms that provide contractual data protection guarantees.
- The organization must maintain a documented list of approved AI platforms, along with their approved use cases and data handling guarantees.
- Personnel must verify that a platform is on the approved list before submitting any organizational data to it.

### Development Tool Exception

- AI-enabled development tools integrated into approved software development platforms (e.g., code completion tools, code review assistants) are permitted for software development activities.
- These tools must never be used to process Customer Personally Identifiable Information (PII) as defined in the Data Protection Policy.
- Internal algorithms, anonymized log traces, and business logic are considered "code assets," not Customer PII, for the purpose of this exception.

### Production Infrastructure Exception

- AI services deployed within the organization's production infrastructure (e.g., cloud provider AI/ML services within a controlled VPC) are permitted for customer data processing, subject to existing contractual agreements and security controls.

### Financial Compliance

- The knowing purchase of or subscription to unauthorized AI platforms using organizational payment methods is prohibited and not eligible for reimbursement.
- Accidental or mistaken charges from a vendor must be managed through standard financial reconciliation and are not considered a policy breach.

### Agreement and Training

- All Personnel must read, understand, and comply with this AI Usage Policy.
- Ongoing security awareness training must include safe AI use practices.
- Policy breaches will follow the disciplinary process outlined in the Acceptable Use Policy. Determination of "serious misconduct" must weigh factors such as intent and impact.

## Compliance and Enforcement

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action as outlined in the Acceptable Use Policy and Information Security Policy.
